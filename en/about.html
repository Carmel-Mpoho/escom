Our mission

Eric Shimba COMPANY, ESCOM was created in Lubumbashi in July 2020, by ERIC MWADJANVITA SHIMBA.

Our mission is to :

  • Provide administrative and technical support to companies and organizations
  • Enable companies and organizations to save time by focusing on their core business relieve companies and organizations of administrative and logistical headaches
  • Provide companies with files of technically-adequate manpower to meet their objectives
  • To enable companies and organizations to benefit rapidly from the services of expatriate manpower (in line with skills) by processing and obtaining residence and work permits quickly.
  • Facilitate rapid social and professional integration for all expatriates as soon as they enter the country.

Our clients

METC ENGINEERING

We are actively involved in the construction of the kipushi KICO SA zinc processing plant (a subsidiary of the Ivanhoe Group), providing the South African EPCM company METC ENGINEERING with the all Congolese workforce, comprising engineers in various fields: Construction engineers, Materials Control, Plant Process Sparing Identification Engineers, Electrical engineers, Tailing Supervision Facility, Safety Officers, Site Secretary, Drivers, Cleaners, etc.

ANDRITZ HYDRO

We are providing to ANDRITZ HYDRO, (company specializes in the construction of turbines and the rehabilitation of hydroelectric power plants) visa and Congolese work permit facilitation services, airport and port travel protocol (formalities) for expatriate staff, and logistics for the Mwadingusha hydroelectric power plant rehabilitation project in Katanga province.
